~ ~ ~ Z E N K A I ~ ~ ~

A relaxing exploration game

Github

Our game, Zenkai, is a simple climbing game where players are encouraged to explore and climb to collect wisps, allowing them to get upgrades. It is intended to be relaxing and fulfilling, while being a balance between easy and challenging. The game may be simple, but we believe that it still meets every aspect we wanted from it, though there are some additions we are planning on making to help make it an even better experience.
